# Settings and Onboarding

Settings and onboarding should help a contractor configure the business without feeling like setup work is endless.

## Organization Settings

Planned settings include company profile, logo, service areas, tax settings, proposal defaults, invoice defaults, payment settings, file storage policy, roles, and notification preferences.

## Onboarding

Initial onboarding should collect:

- Company name and service area.
- Primary service lines.
- Team roles.
- Default tax and payment assumptions.
- Sample customer, lead, estimate, and job data for guided setup.

## UX Direction

Use progress, defaults, and short forms. Avoid blocking the dashboard behind a long wizard. Owners should reach a useful workspace quickly and refine settings over time.
