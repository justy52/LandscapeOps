# CRM

The CRM should help contractors follow up quickly and preserve property context. It should track prospects, customers, account relationships, communication history, next actions, and lead source performance.

## Initial Capabilities

- Lead capture with name, property, source, budget, service interest, and urgency.
- Customer profiles with residential or commercial details.
- Pipeline statuses from new inquiry through won or lost.
- Assigned owner and next action dates.
- Notes that distinguish sales notes from production notes.

## UX Direction

CRM views should favor fast scanning: hot leads, overdue follow-up, proposal-ready leads, and inactive opportunities. Mobile should let an estimator update status after a site visit without digging through dense forms.

## Later Integrations

Resend and Twilio can support automated follow-up reminders after the core permission and audit model is implemented.
