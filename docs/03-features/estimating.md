# Estimating

Estimating turns qualified opportunities into priced work with enough structure to protect margin and support production handoff.

## Initial Capabilities

- Estimate records tied to organization, customer, optional lead, and optional job.
- Status flow: draft, internal review, sent, approved, declined, expired.
- Totals stored in cents for subtotal, tax, and total.
- Margin percentage stored for review and reporting.
- Files for proposal PDFs, plans, photos, and scope attachments.

## Future Requirements

Later phases should add line items, assemblies, labor rates, material catalog, equipment, subcontractor costs, markups, discounts, taxes, and proposal templates.

## Guardrails

Approved estimates should become source-of-truth inputs for contracts, job budgets, and invoice schedules. Any change after approval must be versioned or captured as a change order.
