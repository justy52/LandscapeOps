# Field Operations

Field operations should let crews update work from the job site without needing office-heavy workflows.

## Initial Direction

- Mobile-first job view.
- Daily notes and completion status.
- Photos tied to customer, job, lead, estimate, or invoice where appropriate.
- Punch list items and blockers.
- Crew accountability through timestamps and actor ids.

## Field UX

The field interface should use large touch targets, clear status states, resilient empty states, and minimal typing. It should work well outdoors on phones.

## Future Considerations

Offline support, location-aware check-ins, crew timesheets, material usage, and equipment tracking should be evaluated after the core job model is stable.
