# Product Spec

LandscapeOps is a premium operations platform for landscape contractors that need one command center for sales, production, field execution, and profitability. The product serves owners, operations managers, estimators, account managers, office admins, crew leads, and eventually clients.

## Core Promise

Contractors should be able to see what is coming in, what has been sold, what crews are doing today, what is ready to bill, and which jobs are profitable without stitching together spreadsheets, texts, email, accounting exports, and photo folders.

## Primary Workflows

- Capture leads with source, property, budget, urgency, and next action.
- Convert qualified leads into estimates and proposals.
- Move approved proposals into contracts and jobs.
- Schedule crews, visits, equipment, and subcontractors.
- Capture field notes, photos, punch items, and completion status.
- Generate invoices, track payments, and monitor overdue AR.
- Report on conversion, revenue, job margin, labor pressure, and profitability.

## Product Principles

- Every screen should help a contractor make an operational decision.
- Mobile should be first-class for field and owner usage.
- Financial information must be auditable and clearly sourced.
- Tenant isolation must be designed in from the first schema.
- The interface should feel premium, calm, and specific to landscape work.
